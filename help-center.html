Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my machinery on MechSwap?

To list your machinery, sign in to your account and navigate to the "Sell Machinery" page. Fill in the required details about your equipment, upload photos, and submit your listing for review.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, UPI, and secure escrow services for high-value transactions. Contact us for more details.

How do I verify my seller account?

After signing up, complete your profile with business details and upload verification documents. Our team will review and approve your account within 2-3 business days.

Is there a fee for listing machinery?

Basic listings are free. Premium listings with enhanced visibility are available for a small fee. Contact our sales team for custom pricing plans.
