About MechSwap

India's leading marketplace for industrial machinery

Our Mission

At MechSwap, we're on a mission to revolutionize the industrial machinery marketplace in India. We believe that every business deserves access to quality equipment at fair prices, and every seller deserves a transparent platform to reach genuine buyers.

Our platform bridges the gap between buyers and sellers of new and used industrial machinery, making transactions seamless, secure, and efficient. We're committed to building trust in an industry that has long needed modernization.

By leveraging technology and deep industry expertise, we're creating an ecosystem where machinery trading is as easy as buying or selling any other product online.

What We Do

  • Connect verified sellers with genuine buyers across India
  • Provide transparent pricing and detailed machinery specifications
  • Offer secure payment options and escrow services for high-value transactions
  • Deliver nationwide logistics and inspection services
  • Maintain a verified seller program for trust and credibility
  • Provide 24/7 customer support for all your machinery needs
  • Offer financing options for qualified buyers
  • Facilitate machinery inspection and certification services
